CrystalsCollector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your local score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total number goes above the random number

Your game will hide this amount until the player clicks a crystal.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
